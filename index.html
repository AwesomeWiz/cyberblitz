Welcome to CyberBlitz

CyberBlitz, the vibrant tech club of Rajagiri School of Engineering and Technology's Computer Science Department, is your gateway to innovation and collaboration. We host coding challenges, tech workshops, and guest lectures to boost your skills and knowledge. Join us to explore cutting-edge technology, work on exciting projects, and connect with like-minded peers. Dive into the future of tech with CyberBlitz and make your mark in the world of computer science!
